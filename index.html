About

Who am I?

My name is Andrew Frolkin, and I am studying computer science at the University of Waterloo. I'm passionate about just about anything related to computers and technology. I have always been interested in how computers work, and this curiosity has led me to discover not only the usefulness of technology, but also its limitations.

A little bit about myself.

My primary interest is in software design and development, but I enjoy learning about all kinds of different topics in computer science. I've worked with ancient languages like Business Basic, and also more modern ones like HTML5 and C++. If it has something to do with computer science, chances are I'm interested in learning about it!

And here's some more.

Other than programming, I also enjoy graphic design, photography, traveling, videogames, and movies. Every now and then, I'll devote my time to a personal project, my most recent project was developing this website. If I'm not studying, working or watching movies, I'll usually be spending my time with my friends or family!
